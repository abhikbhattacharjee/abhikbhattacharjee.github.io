Level Up!

Each year, MIT-WPU's Computer Department arranges a two-day free workshop on various topics for aspiring engineers of various fields, with the sole purpose of "Sharing Knowledge, Bridging Gaps". This year our topic "Level Up!" aims at introducing the participants with the concepts related to Game Development.
