LINUXICATION 2020

Linuxication is a basic event oraganised by a group of zealous students with an objective to teach others the basics of linux. It encompasses sets of sessions organised and taken by students.

Linuxication is a three day workshop educating the masses about Linux and open source concepts from the grounds up. The workshop is conducted at a very nominal fee of Rs. 300/-. We are excited to bridge the gap between you and Linux. Do join us for this hands-on 3 day workshop
